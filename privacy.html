Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Welcome to Prompster AI. We respect your digital privacy and are committed to protecting it through compliance with this data protection strategy policy structure.

Prompster operates entirely as a front-end client-side script utility framework. We do not maintain off-site cloud databases, nor do we collect, store, log or track any custom user string text parameters or creative prompt histories generated inside our panel fields.

Third-party marketing vendors, including Google AdSense, use device cookies to serve contextual advertising assets based on a user's prior trajectories across this or alternative internet websites.

Users can optimize or opt out of personalized advertising matrices at any point by visiting Google Ads Settings or updating their localized web browser profile properties.

3. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

We completely align with global user data protection regulations. Since our script framework does not harvest, scrape, or retain any personally identifiable information (PII), your security baseline remains absolute.
